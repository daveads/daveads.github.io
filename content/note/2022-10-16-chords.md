### Piano Chord formation 

```
b :- half note down
# :- half note up
```

* Maj ::
**1 - 3 - 5**


* 6th or Maj6th ::
**1 - 3 - 5- 6**



* M7 or Maj7th ::
**1 - 3 - 5 - 7**



* M7#11 ::
**1- 3 - 5 - 7 - #11**


* add9 ::
**1 - 3 - 5 - 9**


* M7<sup>9</sup> ::
**1 - 3 - 7 - 9**


* 6^9^ ::
**1 - 3 - 5 - 6 - 9**


* aug ::
**1 - 3 - #5**

* min ::
**1 - b3 - 5**


* m6 minor6 ::
**1 - b3 - 5 - 6**


* m7  ::
**1 - b3 - 5 - b7**

* m7<sup>b5</sup> half diminish ::
**1 - b3 - 5 - b7**

* madd9 ::
**1 - b3 - 5 - 9**

* m7<sup>9</sup> ::
**1 - b3 - 5 - b7 - 9**

* m7<sup>11</sup> ::
**1 - b3 - 5 - b7 - 11**

* mM7 minorMaj7th ::
**1 - b3 - 5 - 7**

* mM7<sup>9</sup> ::
**1 - b3 - 5 - 7 - 9**

* dim :: 
**1 - b3 - b5**

* dim7 ::
**1 - b3 - b5 - 6**

* 7 or dominat chord
**1 - 3 - 5 - b7**

* 7sus4 ::
**1 - 3 - 4 - 5 - b7**

* 7<sup>b5</sup> ::
**1 - 3 - b5 - b7**

* 7<sup>9</sup> ::
**1 - 3 - 5 - b7 - 9**

* 7#11 ::
**1 - 3 - 5 - b7 - #11**

* 7<sup>13</sup> ::
**1 - 3 - 5 - b7 - 13**
