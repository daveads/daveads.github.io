Piano Chord formation

b :- half note down
# :- half note up

  • Maj :: 1 - 3 - 5

  • 6th or Maj6th :: 1 - 3 - 5- 6

  • M7 or Maj7th :: 1 - 3 - 5 - 7

  • M7#11 :: 1- 3 - 5 - 7 - #11

  • add9 :: 1 - 3 - 5 - 9

  • M79 :: 1 - 3 - 7 - 9

  • 6^9^ :: 1 - 3 - 5 - 6 - 9

  • aug :: 1 - 3 - #5

  • min :: 1 - b3 - 5

  • m6 minor6 :: 1 - b3 - 5 - 6

  • m7 :: 1 - b3 - 5 - b7

  • m7b5 half diminish :: 1 - b3 - 5 - b7

  • madd9 :: 1 - b3 - 5 - 9

  • m79 :: 1 - b3 - 5 - b7 - 9

  • m711 :: 1 - b3 - 5 - b7 - 11

  • mM7 minorMaj7th :: 1 - b3 - 5 - 7

  • mM79 :: 1 - b3 - 5 - 7 - 9

  • dim :: 1 - b3 - b5

  • dim7 :: 1 - b3 - b5 - 6

  • 7 or dominat chord 1 - 3 - 5 - b7

  • 7sus4 :: 1 - 3 - 4 - 5 - b7

  • 7b5 :: 1 - 3 - b5 - b7

  • 79 :: 1 - 3 - 5 - b7 - 9

  • 7#11 :: 1 - 3 - 5 - b7 - #11

  • 713 :: 1 - 3 - 5 - b7 - 13
